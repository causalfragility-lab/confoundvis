# confoundvis 0.1.0

* First release on CRAN.
* Provides visualization tools for sensitivity analysis to unmeasured
  confounding in observational studies.
* Includes contour-based sensitivity plots, robustness curves, Love-plot
  style displays, and local Taylor approximation graphics.
* Supports three sensitivity frameworks: impact threshold (Frank, 2000),
  partial R-squared methods (Cinelli & Hazlett, 2020), and E-value style
  metrics (VanderWeele & Ding, 2017).
